Overview

The Nimbus II EMS pump boasts a small form packed with many features. The single channel linear peristaltic design effictively moves fluid from resevoir to delivery site regardless of gravity or position. The 12 protocol library allows for flexible customization and various safeguards. Our weight based and continuous modes were made specifically for EMS. The pump can be powered via the micro-usb port as well as from the internal battery for up to 10 days of running infusion or 1500mL. You also have the ability to spike any standard IV bag and download a full event history from the pump.
